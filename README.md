# 💫 About Me:
I'm an M.S. Computer Science student at University of Colorado Boulder, specializing in robotics and machine learning. I work in the HIRO Lab building perception and control systems, and I spend most of my time closing the gap between research and production-level robotics — clean code, real metrics, end-to-end pipelines.
<br><br>
🔭 I’m currently working on a multi-sensor 3D object detection system on nuScenes using LiDAR (PointPillars) and camera-LiDAR fusion, with full BEV representation and mAP/NDS evaluation.
<br><br>
👯 I’m looking to collaborate on robotics perception, autonomous systems, and anything at the intersection of computer vision and real-world robot deployment.
<br><br>
🌱 I’m currently learning sensor fusion architectures, BEV-based 3D detection, and production-level perception pipelines for autonomous driving.
<br><br>
💬 Ask me about reinforcement learning for continuous control, RGB-D tracking systems in ROS2, or getting robotics projects done without expensive hardware.
<br><br>
⚡ Fun fact I trained an SAC agent to run MuJoCo HalfCheetah to a mean reward of 7,648, from random flailing to smooth locomotion in under an hour on a laptop GPU.
